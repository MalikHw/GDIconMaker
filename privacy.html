🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

TL;DR: We don't store your data. Your images are processed temporarily and immediately deleted. No tracking, no accounts, no BS.

What We Collect

Short answer: Nothing permanently.

  • When you upload an image, it's temporarily stored on our server for processing (less than 5 seconds)
  • After your icon pack is generated, all uploaded images and temporary files are automatically deleted
  • We count total visits and icons made for the counter (anonymous, no personal data)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No user accounts
  • ❌ No email addresses
  • ❌ No IP addresses stored
  • ❌ No cookies for tracking
  • ❌ No analytics software (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • ❌ No databases

How Your Images Are Handled

  1. You upload an image → Temporarily stored on server
  2. PHP processes it (resize, rotate, place on template)
  3. ZIP file created in /downloads/ folder
  4. You download the ZIP
  5. ZIP file is automatically deleted after download
  6. All temporary files deleted immediately

Data Retention

We retain ZERO personal data. The only things that persist are:

  • Anonymous visit counter (just a number, no user info)
  • Anonymous icons created counter (just a number, no user info)

Third-Party Services

We don't use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

The only external links are:

  • Nerd Fonts CDN (for icons)
  • Social media links (YouTube, Twitch, GitHub, donation page)

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. You're free to use the tool anonymously.

Changes to This Policy
